## Mindset

"Every piece of data is a clue"

- Pic reuse
- Friends/family circle
- What can I learn from their images/videos
  - Backgrounds
  - exif
- See targets mental state
- Public records
- A person's characteristics
  - Tattoos/scars
  - piercings
  - glasses
  - habits like smoking
  - gait
  - language patterns (verbal or online)
